1 - Register Before using the application.

2 - login with the registered username and password.

3 - On Successful login you will be redireted to home page of the IBIBS banking application

4 - you can update your profile related details by clicking on Profile navigation and then by clicking Edit Button.

5 - If you delete your account, you will be logged out automatically from the system.

5 - To apply Loan click on Loans navigation.

6 - you can click logout button on top navigation to sign off from the application.

7 - Required field validation covered, some of the validation is not covered due to time constraint. 

Note :  Application uses fake backend localstorage for saving its data.
